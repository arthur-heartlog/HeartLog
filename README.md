# HeartLog - A Private Cardiac Health Companion

**HeartLog** is a minimalist and secure iOS application designed for specialized heart risk assessment and daily health logging. Using a scientific evaluation engine, it helps you translate abstract physical feelings into intuitive, actionable health data.

---

## 🌟 Key Features

- **Real-Time Risk Dashboard**: Monitor your heart risk score based on energy levels, heart sensations (palpitation frequency), and sleep quality.
-  **Deep Medical Insights**: Visualize your cardiac trends over 7, 30, or 90 days with native SwiftUI Charts.
-  **Structured Health History**: Maintain a comprehensive log of your daily check-ins to provide detailed data for future clinical consultations.
-  **Privacy-First Design**: **100% Local Storage.** All physiological benchmarks and logs are saved exclusively on your iPhone; no data ever leaves your device.
-  **Personalized Benchmarking**: Evaluation logic dynamically adjusts based on your age, gender, height, and weight to ensure accuracy.

---

## 🛠 Technology Stack

- **UI Framework**: SwiftUI
- **Data Persistence**: SwiftData (Local-Only)
-  **Architecture**: MVVM
-  **Compatibility**: iOS 17.0+

## 🔒 Privacy & Security
