
# Vertex Sudoku - A Premium Logic Masterpiece | 幻方数独 - 极致逻辑美学之作

**Vertex Sudoku** is a minimalist and elegant iOS application designed for the ultimate Sudoku experience. By combining high-end "Glassmorphism" design with a hardcore logic engine, it transforms every puzzle into a journey of visual and mental harmony.

**Vertex Sudoku** 是一款极简且优雅的 iOS 应用，专为极致的数独体验而生。通过将高级感“玻璃拟态”设计与硬核逻辑引擎相结合，它将每一道谜题挑战都转化为一场视觉与思维的和谐之旅。

---

## 🌟 Key Features | 核心功能

### [English]
- **Curated Logic Paths**: Challenge yourself with 400 handcrafted levels across 4 difficulty tiers: Easy, Medium, Hard, and Expert.
- **Dynamic Scoring System**: Earn "Total Points" based on your speed and accuracy. Track your evolution from a Newbie to a Logic Legend.
- **Deep Performance Analytics**: Visualize your solving trends and success rates with comprehensive statistics.
- **Tactile Immersion**: Experience premium haptic feedback and fluid micro-animations that make every move feel alive.
- **Privacy-First Design**: **100% Local Storage.** Your progress, scores, and puzzles are saved exclusively on your device. No cloud tracking.

### [中文]
- **精选逻辑路径**：挑战从简单到专家 4 个难度的 400 道独立关卡，循序渐进磨练您的逻辑思维。
- **动态积分系统**：根据用时与准确率积累“总积分”。见证您从“数独新手”到“逻辑传奇”的进阶历程。
- **深度表现分析**：通过详尽的统计数据，可视化您的解题趋势、胜率以及各难度分布。
- **沉浸式触感交互**：适配高级 Haptic 触觉反馈与丝滑微动画，让每一次填入、擦除都拥有极致的物理手感。
- **隐私至上设计**：**100% 本地存储。** 您的进度、积分和记录仅保存在您的设备上，数据永不离设备，无任何第三方追踪。
