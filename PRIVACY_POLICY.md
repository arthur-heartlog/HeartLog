# Privacy Policy for HeartLog

**Effective Date: April 1, 2026**

Your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy describes how **HeartLog** (the "App") handles your personal information.

---

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
**HeartLog does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal or health data on external servers.** 
All information you enter into the App, including but not limited to:
- Age, gender, height, and weight.
- Heart risk logs and symptoms.
- Sleep and energy scores.

**Remains exclusively on your device.** We have no access to your data, and we do not sell or share it with any third parties.

## 2. Data Storage
The App uses Apple's native **SwiftData** framework to store your information locally on your iPhone. This data is protected by iOS's built-in security features and encryption.

## 3. Third-Party Services
HeartLog does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. There is no cloud synchronization or external API communication (except for internal version checks via the official Apple iTunes API).

## 4. User Control and Data Deletion
Since all data is stored locally, you have full control over it:
- You can edit or delete individual logs within the App.
- To delete all data associated with HeartLog, simply uninstall the App from your device. Once uninstalled, all local data is permanently wiped.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in an updated version of the App or its documentation.
