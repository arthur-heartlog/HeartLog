
# TideMoon - A Premium Menstrual Tracker | 汐月 - 极致优雅的私人经期管家

**TideMoon** is a minimalist and elegant iOS application designed for the modern woman who values both functionality and aesthetics. By combining high-end "Glassmorphism" design with precision tracking algorithms, it transforms menstrual health management into a journey of visual and habitual harmony.

**TideMoon (汐月)** 是一款极简且优雅的 iOS 应用，专为追求功能与美学共存的现代女性而生。通过将高级感“玻璃拟态”设计与精准追踪算法相结合，它将经期管理转化为一场视觉与生活习惯的和谐之旅。

---

## 🌟 Key Features | 核心功能

### [English]
- **Intelligent Tide Tracking**: Log your period with a single tap. The dynamic "Tide Ring" visualizes your current cycle day and phase in real-time.
- **Deep Cycle Analytics**: Unlock comprehensive insights into your cycle length, variance, and fertility trends after just two recordings.
- **Professional Data Export**: Generate clean, medical-grade PDF reports with one click—perfect for healthcare consultations or personal records.
- **Security & Privacy**: **100% Local Storage.** All sensitive data is saved exclusively on your device via SwiftData. Secured by FaceID/TouchID. Zero cloud tracking.
- **Micro-Animation Immersion**: Experience fluid animations and premium haptic feedback that make the app feel alive and responsive to your touch.

### [中文]
- **智能潮汐追踪**：一键开启/结束经期记录。灵动的“潮汐环”实时可视化您当前所处的周期天数与四个生理阶段。
- **深度周期分析**：仅需两次记录即可解锁详尽的周期长度趋势、波动分析以及基于本地算法的受孕概率预测分布。
- **专业数据导出**：一键生成排版精美的医疗级 PDF 健康报告，是您就医咨询或整理个人健康档案的有力助手。
- **隐私至上设计**：**100% 本地存储。** 所有敏感数据仅通过 SwiftData 保存在您的设备本地。支持 FaceID 安全锁定，数据由您掌控，零第三方追踪。
- **沉浸式交互体验**：适配丝滑的微动画与高级 Haptic 触觉反馈，让每一次记录、滑动都拥有极致的温润感与物理反馈。
